## Feedback Guidelines

### **For Continuing (when solution needs improvement):**
- **Be specific**: Point out exactly where errors occur
- **Be constructive**: Suggest concrete improvements and alternative approaches
- **Be educational**: Explain why certain steps are problematic
- **Guide thinking**: Help redirect toward better solution paths
- **Stay focused**: Address the most critical issues first

### **For Ending (when solution is acceptable):**
- **Acknowledge strengths**: Highlight what was done well
- **Note any minor issues**: Mention small improvements that could be made
- **Confirm correctness**: Briefly validate the key reasoning steps
- **Keep it concise**: No need for extensive feedback if the solution is good
